About me

Hello, I'm Edda, an Icelandic Multimedia Design student at Zealand Academy, specializing in UX Research, Visual Design, and Project Management. My approach to design is methodical and informed. I use my completed BA in Anthropology and Master's in Tourism to deeply analyze human challenges and translate those insights into clear, innovative digital solutions.

How I work

Great digital experiences emerge from a methodology rooted in empathy and rigorous teamwork. My background ensures every project is grounded in genuine user understanding and sound reasoning. Maintaining an efficient workflow necessitates open communication and methodical structures. Ultimately, my focus is on contributing to solutions that are visually balanced, functionally clear, and authentically reflect human behavior.

What I can do

I am a highly versatile team member, contributing across the full product lifecycle. My strategic analysis skills blend with hands-on execution, covering deep UX Research, prototyping, UI, and technical Front-End Coding (HTML/SCSS/JS). I maintain a calm, methodical mindset throughout every task. This blend of creative ideas and technical skills helps the team deliver meaningful results.

Have a project or idea? I’m just a LinkedIn connection away.

Whether product, content, or multidisciplinary work, I'm ready to help.
