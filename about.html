About me

Hello, I'm Edda — a multimedia design student at Zealand, currently in my third semester. I specialize in UX research, information architecture, and project management, turning messy challenges into clear, user-centered solutions.

How I work

I believe great digital experiences come from clarity, empathy, and teamwork. In practice, that means thorough user research, thoughtful IA, and transparent coordination that keeps everyone aligned and valued. My tagline, From complexity to clarity, guides every project I undertake.

What I do

I'm open to a wide range of digital design work, from UX research and information architecture to UI, magazine design, prototyping, and beyond. Whether it's a product interface, a content-driven platform, or a multidisciplinary project, I bring a calm, methodical approach, strong collaboration, and a focus on delivering meaningful results.
