About me

Hello, I'm Edda — a multimedia design student at Zealand Academy of Technologies and Business, currently in my third semester. I specialize in UX research, visual design and project management, turning complex challenges into clear, human-centered solutions.

How I work

I believe great digital experiences come from clarity, empathy, and teamwork. To me, that means understanding real user needs, building thoughtful structures, and keeping collaboration open and honest. I love creating clean, visually balanced interfaces that are grounded in research and reflect how people actually think and interact.

What I can do

I enjoy working across a wide range of digital design - from UX research and information architecture to UI, magazine design, prototyping, and coding. I tend to find something interesting and challenging in everything I do, and I like combining creativity and technical skills to bring ideas to life. I approach projects with a calm, methodical mindset, enjoy collaborating closely with others, and focus on creating work that delivers meaningful results.

Have a project or idea? I’m just a LinkedIn connection away.

Whether product, content, or multidisciplinary work, I'm ready to help.
