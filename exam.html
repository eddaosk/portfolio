What we've built together

Two recent projects where we were the same team. Here's what we did — and how we think.

SaaS · Vending machine · Sustainability

JuicOrganic

An organic juice company wanted to go from B2B to B2C. We designed a smart vending machine concept with a touchscreen ordering system, a reusable bottle with barcode rewards, and a full SaaS management layer.

  • User interviews, personas, journey maps, A/B testing
  • Design system + high-fidelity Figma prototype with variables
  • Coded front-end (HTML, SCSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap)
  • Campaign video (After Effects) + SoMe content plan
View coded prototype →
EdTech · Neuro-inclusive · AI

GradeAid — The Deep Blue

An AI learning platform needed a UX layer designed for neurodivergent children. We developed an ocean-themed concept grounded in Blue Mind theory — calming colors, non-human avatars, and age-adapted interfaces that reduce sensory overload.

  • Neuro-inclusive design research (WCAG, color science, Blue Mind)
  • Character design + age-adapted visual identity (kids → teens)
  • Figma prototype tested with an 8-year-old + expert validation
  • Business Model Canvas, Value Proposition Canvas, animations
