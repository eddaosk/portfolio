My Projects

JuicOrganic

SaaS solution

A SaaS concept designed to help JuicOrganic shift from B2B to B2C through a smart vending machine that encourages eco-friendly habits. My role focused on UX research, information architecture, and front-end development using JavaScript, HTML, SCSS, and Bootstrap, as well as creating promotional motion graphics. The result is a user-centred prototype that merges sustainability with digital interaction. View the coded prototype (optimised for iPad Pro)

Econiq

FinTech SaaS Website Prototype & Brand Identity

For this FinTech startup, I served as project owner and UX lead, tasked with developing a brand identity and user-centred website prototype. The final prototype offers a streamlined, accessible interface that effectively guides users toward conversion
